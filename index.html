Definition

Suicide refers to the dangerous behavior that an individual intentionally or voluntarily takes various means to end his life under the influence of complex psychological activities. Suicide is a complex social phenomenon. Suicide is not a disease, that is, there is no disease called suicide, suicide is a kind of personal behavior caused by disease or other reasons. At the same time, ICD-10, which serves as a disease guideline, does not have the diagnosis of suicide.

What is suicide?

It is a kind of conscious self destructing behavior that ends one's life with one's own will or means.
